Game Rules

"Why Me?!" is a hilarious party game that puts your assumptions and perceptions to the test!
Gather your friends, grab your favorite drinks, and get ready for some laughter-filled moments.
To play, each player takes turns drawing a card featuring a quirky statement or scenario.
It could be anything from "Who's always glued to their phone?" to "Who's most likely to conquer the world?"
Once the card is revealed, it's up to the group to decide who among them best fits the description.
You can either take turns assigning the card to someone or,
for a more interactive twist, count down from three and point to the person you think fits the bill.
Majority rules!
But here's the catch: the chosen person must then take a sip of their drink for each card assigned to them.
So, whether you're being praised for your quirks or gently roasted for your habits,
every round is guaranteed to bring laughter and camaraderie.
With its simple yet entertaining gameplay, "Why Me?!" is the perfect icebreaker for any gathering,
ensuring a night filled with fun memories and inside jokes.
