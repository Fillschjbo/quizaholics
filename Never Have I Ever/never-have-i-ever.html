Game Rules

1. Players: You need at least three people to play.

2. Start: The person with the app reads the question, like "Never have I ever ridden a horse."

3. Action: If you've done what the person said, you admit it by raising your hand, taking a drink

4. Repeat: After you're done with the question, go to the next.

5. End: There's no specific end point. You can stop whenever you want or keep playing for as long as you're having fun.

Be Honest and Respectful: It's all about having a good time, so be honest but also respectful of each other's comfort levels.

That's it! It's a simple game that's all about learning interesting things about your friends while having a laugh. Enjoy!
